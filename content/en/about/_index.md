## Mission

The Asia Agrarian Research Institute is dedicated to studying 
agricultural and rural issues across Asia, contributing to 
sustainable development through cross-border research networks 
and information sharing.

## Vision

We aim to serve as a hub for agrarian research in Asia — 
translating, summarizing, and sharing research and policy 
information across Korean, English, Japanese, Spanish, and 
other languages, connecting researchers, policymakers, and 
civil society worldwide.

## Research Areas

- Asian agricultural policy and food sovereignty
- Rural community governance
- International political economy of agriculture
- Sustainable agriculture and food systems
- Rural basic services and social economy
